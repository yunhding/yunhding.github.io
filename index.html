Research Interests
Theoretical studies of spacetime symmetries, with primary interest in testing Lorentz and CPT symmetry in precision experiments, including theory of Lorentz- and CPT-violating quantum electrodynamics, particle and antiparticle comparisons in Penning traps, corrections to particle's spin precession in Electric Dipole Moment experiments, and Lorentz and CPT tests in atomic, molecular, and optical systems.
